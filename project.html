Luminary Finance App

Client Luminary Inc.
Year 2024
Role UI/UX Designer
Duration 8 weeks

Project Overview

Luminary Finance needed a complete redesign of their mobile app. The goal was to simplify complex financial data into an intuitive dashboard that anyone can understand at a glance. Working closely with their product team, we transformed cluttered screens into a clean, modern experience that reduced user confusion and increased daily active usage by 40%.

The Process

01

Discovery & Research

User interviews, competitor analysis, and stakeholder workshops to define the problem space and success metrics.

02

Wireframes & IA

Rapid sketching and digital wireframing to explore information architecture and key user flows before committing to visuals.

03

Visual Design

High-fidelity mockups with a cohesive design system — typography, color, spacing, components — all documented for the dev team.

04

Prototype & Test

Interactive prototypes tested with real users. Three rounds of iteration based on usability testing feedback.
